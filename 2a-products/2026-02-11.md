# 2A Products & Services Intel — 11 Feb 2026

## Summary
The Sig P320 safety saga intensifies with a Bloomberg deep-dive and The Trace follow-up, plus Florida legislation that could shield manufacturers from lawsuits. Post-SHOT Show 2026 dust is settling — Glock Gen 6 family and the Staccato HD C4X are the handgun stories with legs. The $200 NFA excise tax on suppressors and SBRs is officially dead as of 01 Jan 2026 via the "Big Beautiful Bill." Training side: Greg Ellifritz at Active Response Training is promoting a new class concept worth tracking.

## Legislative / Industry

- **$200 NFA Excise Tax Eliminated** — As of 01 Jan 2026, the $200 tax on suppressors and SBRs is gone via the "Big Beautiful Bill." NSSF background checks for NFA items exploded in January. Expect continued SBR/suppressor product launches capitalizing on this. [News From The States](https://www.newsfromthestates.com/article/which-parts-big-beautiful-bill-will-go-effect-2026) | [Colion Noir](https://www.mrcolionnoir.com/nssf-background-checks-for-nfa-items-exploded-in-january/)

- **Freedom From Unfair Gun Taxes Act** — New federal bill to prohibit state/local excise taxes on firearms, ammo, and components. [NSSF](https://www.nssf.org/articles/freedom-from-unfair-gun-taxes-act-aims-to-stop-punishing-taxes/)

- **Virginia Gun Control Package** — Rammed through the House (09 Feb). [Colion Noir](https://www.mrcolionnoir.com/virginia-lawmakers-ram-gun-control-package-through-house/)

The NFA tax death plus Glock Gen 6 dropping at the same time is going to make 2026 a year where people actually spend money instead of just window shopping. The P320 saga is the industry's biggest unforced error in a decade — Sig's play to legislate away liability instead of fixing the problem tells you everything you need to know about where their priorities are.
